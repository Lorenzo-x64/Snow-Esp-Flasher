02 · How it works

Four steps from a blank ESP32 to a running transceiver with its Wi-Fi dashboard up.

01 · Plug in

Connect USB

Plug your ESP32 into your computer with a USB-C data cable.

02 · Choose

Pick a firmware

Click Install on the TX, RX, or RX Lite card that matches your board.

03 · Flash

Select the port

Choose the serial port in the browser dialog. The chip is erased and the firmware + dashboard written.

04 · Connect

Join the Wi-Fi

Connect to Snow Transceiver (password 1234567890) and open 192.168.4.1.

03 · Requirements

What you need.

Anything that speaks Web Serial — most modern Chromium browsers on desktop will do.

Hardware

uPesy ESP32 WROOM 32E

Or any ESP32 with 4 MB of flash. A USB-C data cable connected to your computer.

Browser

Chrome, Edge, or Opera

Web Serial isn't supported in Firefox or Safari — use a Chromium-based browser on desktop.

Drivers

CP2102 or CH340

Most systems pick these up automatically. If your port doesn't appear, install the driver for your board.

04 · Troubleshooting

When things stall.

The usual suspects, and how to get past them.

Install button is greyed out

Switch to Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop. Web Serial isn't available in mobile or Safari/Firefox.

No serial port appears

Install the CP2102 or CH340 USB driver for your board, then reload this page.

Stuck at "connecting"

Hold BOOT on the board, click install, release once it starts erasing.

Dashboard doesn't load after flash

The LittleFS image was included with the firmware — try a hard power cycle and reconnect to the Wi-Fi.
