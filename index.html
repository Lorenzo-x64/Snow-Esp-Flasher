❄ Snow Transceiver

Flash your Snow Transceiver.

Pick the firmware for your board, connect over USB, and flash in seconds. No IDE, no drivers, no terminal.

⚠️ Chrome, Edge, or Opera required. Web Serial isn't supported in Firefox or Safari. Open this page in a Chromium-based browser on desktop.

What you need

  • uPesy ESP32 WROOM 32E (or any ESP32 with 4 MB flash)
  • USB-C cable connected to your computer
  • Chrome, Edge, or Opera — Firefox and Safari don't support Web Serial

How to flash

  1. Plug your ESP32 into USB.
  2. Pick the right firmware card above and click Install.
  3. Select the serial port for your board in the browser dialog.
  4. Click Install when prompted — the chip is erased and firmware + dashboard are written.
  5. When done, the board reboots and broadcasts Wi-Fi Snow Transceiver (password 1234567890).
  6. Connect and open http://192.168.4.1.

Troubleshooting

  • Install button greyed out — switch to Chrome / Edge / Opera on desktop.
  • No serial port appears — install the CP2102 or CH340 USB driver for your board, then reload this page.
  • Stuck at "connecting" — hold BOOT on the board, click install, release once it starts.
  • Dashboard doesn't load after flash — the LittleFS was included; try a hard power cycle.
